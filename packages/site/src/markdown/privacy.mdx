**Effective as of January 19th, 2025**

## Information We Collect

We may collect:

- URL Data: URLs you shorten.
- Usage Data: Information about how you use the Service, such as clicks and analytics.
- Technical Data: Browser type, device information, and operating system.
- Geographic Data: Country, region, city, and time zone (derived from your IP address).
- IP Address Hash: We store a one-way cryptographic hash of your IP address (not the IP itself) to count unique visitors and detect abuse.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide the Service.
- Monitor and improve the Service.

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. However, we may share data:

## Cookies

We only use strictly necessary cookies:

- Purpose: These cookies are essential for the operation of the Service, such as enabling login functionality and maintaining session security.
- No Tracking: We do not use cookies for analytics, advertising, or other non-essential purposes.

You cannot opt out of strictly necessary cookies as they are required for the functionality of the Service. However, we do not store or use these cookies beyond what is required to maintain your login session.
